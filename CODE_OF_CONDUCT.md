# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project expects respectful, technically focused collaboration.

Examples of acceptable behavior:

- giving clear, constructive feedback
- discussing code and product decisions in good faith
- assuming mistakes can be corrected without hostility
- documenting tradeoffs instead of escalating emotionally
- welcoming contributors with different levels of experience

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, insults, or intimidation
- personal attacks in issues, pull requests, or reviews
- discriminatory language or conduct
- spam, trolling, or deliberately disruptive behavior
- sharing private or sensitive information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, lock, or close content that violates these expectations.

Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent loss of participation privileges.

## Reporting

If conduct becomes abusive or disruptive, report it privately to the repository owner rather than escalating publicly in unrelated threads.

## Practical Rule

Raise the technical bar, not the temperature.
